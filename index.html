Mission: Giving Back

I am here on this earth to discover what success means to me and to support my family along the way. I wish to provide a good future for my loved ones and start giving back to those who have helped me on my journey.

Gifts/Skills

Dedication

Gift 1

Dedication is hard work. It means waking up early, sacrificing your free time, getting uncomfortable, taking risks, facing failure, and delving into the unknown. This is not possible without motivation. You need to make the decision to commit. To develop this you need to self reflect and refine your goals and most importantly hold yourself accountable. If you can't do that you won't have motivation to fuel it.

Communication

Gift 2

Communication is a teachable skill. It is something you can grow and develop by practicing things such as simply listening for example. You can grow and develop your communication skills by putting yourself out there, getting comfortable being uncomfortable. Simply talking, whether thats saying hello to someone at the coffee shop or interacting with people on linkedin.

Resourcefulness

Gift 3

Have confidence. Keeping a log of your successes in problem-solving will reinforce confidence in yourself. Instead of adopting a “can-do” attitude, adopt a “will do at any cost” attitude. Don't give up when you can't find an immediate solution or pathway. “Think outside of the box” when looking for solutions to problems. At times you may need to explore options that may not seem logical or the best at the time. Try something different if the first, second or third option is not viable.

Curiosity

Gift 4

Curiosity is a highly valuable skill to develop. It spurs learning and the exchange of ideas. There is a theme to most of these. A good way to develop curiosity as a skill is to get uncomfortable. When you push yourself to do things you never had and really explore that curiosity it helps expand what you are capable of.

Creativity

Gift 5

There are lots of ways to foster creativity. One main and obvious way is to practice. Practice is essential for creativity. Understand that it is not about perfection, there is quality in quantity.

Some jobs that these skills can be utilized in are listed below

  • Software Engineering

    • You need to be curious and creative to succeed in this field. You will be constantly learning, if you don't have the dedication to do so you won't go far.

  • Project Management

    • Strong communication, resourcefullness, and dedication will take you a long way in this position.
